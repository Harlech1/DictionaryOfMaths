plane

A plane is a set of points.It is a flat surface that has no thickness. Each plane divides the space into two parts.

A plane extends without end, even though its drawing appears to have edges.

Through any three noncollinear points there exists exactly one plane.

Two intersecting lines determine exactly one plane. It has two dimensions.

Two parallel lines determine exactly one plane. A line and a point not on that line determine exactly one plane.

If two planes intersect, then they intersect in exactly one line. Two planes that do not intersect are parallel planes.

If two points lie in a plane , then the line containing them lies in the plane.
