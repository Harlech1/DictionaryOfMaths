determinant

Determinants are mathematical objects which are very useful in the analysis and solution of systems of linear equations. Determinants are defined only for square matrices.The determinant of a square matrix AA will be denoted by det(AA) or |AA|. Determinant of order nn consists of n2{\ n}^{2} elements arranged in nn rows and nn columns , and enclosed by two vertical lines. The determinant of any nxnnxn matrix of numbers ,A\ A , is a single number , called detA\ detA\ or det[aij]\det\left\lbrack a_{ij} \right\rbrack. If the determinant of a matrix is 0, the matrix is said to be singular.
