section; cross-section

In 3-D, the cross section of a solid is a plane figure obtained by the intersection of the solid with a plane. It represents an infinitesimal slice of the solid. The cross section of a sphere with a plane is a disk.

The cross section of a cube may be a square, hexagon, or other shape.

The cross section of a right cone may be a circle, an ellipse or a parabola.
