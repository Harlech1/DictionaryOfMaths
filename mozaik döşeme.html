tesselation; tiling

A regular tiling of polygons (in 2-D), polyhedra (in 3-D).

A tessellation is a repeating pattern of figures that completely covers a plane without gaps or overlaps. You can see tessellations in art, architecture, and nature. You can use translations, rotations, and reflections to make a tessellation. There are only three regular tessellations (composed of the hexagon, square and triangle)
