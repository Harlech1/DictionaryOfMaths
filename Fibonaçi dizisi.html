Fibonacci sequence

A famous sequence that is defined recursively and shows up in nature. The first two terms are specified as ao=1a_{o} = 1 and a1=1a_{1} = 1; and other terms are defined as an=an−1+an−2a_{n} = a_{n - 1} + a_{n - 2} , for all n≥2n \geq 2 (each subsequent term is the sum of the two terms immediately preceding it).
