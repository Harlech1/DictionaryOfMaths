dilation with respect to the origin

The meeting point of lines that connect the corresponding points from homothetic figures in a coordinate plane.

The transformation (x,y)→(kx,ky)(x,y) \rightarrow (kx,ky) is a dilation with respect to the origin where kk is the scale factor.

If 0<k<10 < k < 1, the dilation is a reduction. If k>1k > 1, the dilation is an enlargement.
