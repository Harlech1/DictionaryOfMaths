triangle inequality

For any two vectors u→\overrightarrow{u} and v→\overrightarrow{v} : ∥u→+v→∥≤∥u→∥+∥v→∥\left\| \overrightarrow{u} + \overrightarrow{v} \right\|\ \leq \left\| \overrightarrow{u} \right\| + \left\| \overrightarrow{v} \right\|, equality holds iff v→=ku→\overrightarrow{v} = k\overrightarrow{u}\ \ where k≥0k \geq 0, or u→=0→\overrightarrow{u} = \overrightarrow{0}.
