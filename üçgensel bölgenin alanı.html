Comparing areas of triangular regions

a) If two triangles with the same altitude have different bases, the ratio of their areas is the ratio of their bases.

b) If two triangles with the same base have different altitudes, the ratio of their areas is the ratio of their heights.

c) By drawing all three medians of a triangle, we divide the triangle into six regions of equal area.

d) The ratio of areas of similar triangles is the square of the similarity ratio.
