Roman numerals

A system of numerical notations used by the Romans.

Roman numerals are used in the release year for movies and on the numerals on the faces of the watches and clocks.

Roman characters: II: 1, VV: 5, XX: 10, LL: 50, CC: 100, DD: 500, MM: 1000
