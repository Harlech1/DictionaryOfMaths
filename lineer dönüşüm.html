I.T(v1→+v2→)=T(v1→)+T(v2→)T\left( \overrightarrow{v_{1}} + \overrightarrow{v_{2}} \right) = T\left( \overrightarrow{v_{1}} \right) + T(\overrightarrow{v_{2}}) for all vectors v1→\overrightarrow{v_{1}} and v2→\overrightarrow{v_{2}} in VV.
