polyhedron pl. polyhedrons; polyhedra

A polyhedron is a three-dimensional figure whose surfaces are polygons.

It is a closed-space surface which is composed entirely of polygons.

Its faces enclose a single region of space. It is the union of a number of simplexes.

A soccer ball is an irregular polyhedron in which the hexagons and pentagons which make up the surface are all regular polygons.
