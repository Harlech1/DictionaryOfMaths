regular polyhedron

It is a convex polyhedron in which all of the faces are congruent regular polygons, with the same number of

faces meeting at each vertex . They are also called platonic solids after the Greek philosopher Plato.The five

platonic solids are :

regular hexahedron; cube (6 faces , 8 vertices, and 12 edges)

regular tetrahedron; regular triangular pyramid ( 4 faces , 4 vertices, and 6 edges)

regular dodecahedron (12 faces , 20 vertices, and 30 edges)

regular octahedron (8 faces , 6 vertices, and 12 edges)

regular icosahedron (20 faces , 12 vertices, and 30 edges)
