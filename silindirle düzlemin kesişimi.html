cylinder-plane intersection

The intersection of a plane with a right circular cylinder is

I. a circle, if the plane is at right angle to the axis of the cylinder,

II. an ellipse, if the plane is at an acute angle to the axis of the cylinder,

III. a single line if the plane is tangent to the cylinder,

IV. pair of parallel lines if the plane parallel to the axis and cuts the cylinder, or

V. empty set if the plane does not cut the cylinder
