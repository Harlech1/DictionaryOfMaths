ratio

The ratio of one number to another is the quotient when the first is divided by the second and the second number is not zero. The ratio of the number aa to number bb can be written in two ways:

I.As a quotient using a division sign or a colon, a÷ba \div b or a:ba:\ b

II.As a fraction in simplest form ab\frac{a}{b} .

You can use ratios to compare two quantities the measures of which are in the same unit.
