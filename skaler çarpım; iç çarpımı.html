scalar product; inner product; dot product

Scalar product of the vectors u→\overrightarrow{u} and v→\overrightarrow{v} is denoted by u→•v→\overrightarrow{u}\ \bullet \overrightarrow{v} or ⟨u→,v→⟩\left\langle \overrightarrow{u}\ ,\overrightarrow{v} \right\rangle .
