auxiliary circle; circumcircle (of an ellipse)

The circle whose center concurs with that of the ellipse and whose radius is equal to the ellipse’s semimajor axis.

For the ellipse x2a2+y2b2=1\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}} + \frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}} = 1 ,a>ba > b , the equation of the auxiliary circle is x2+y2=a2{x}^{2} + y^{2} = a^{2} .

For the ellipse (x−h)2a2+(y−k)2b2=1\frac{(x - h)^{2}}{a^{2}} + \frac{(y - k)^{2}}{b^{2}} = 1 , a>ba > b , the equation of the auxiliary circle is (x−h)2+(y−k)2=a2(x - h)^{2} + (y - k)^{2} = a^{2}.
