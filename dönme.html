rotation; revolution

A rotation is a transformation in which a figure or coordinate system is turned by an angle about a fixed point, called the center of rotation. A rotation preserves the length of a segment, so it is an isometry.

Rotations can be represented in coordinates. If the point with polar coordinates (r,θ)(r,\theta) is rotated by α\alpha , then the image point will be (r,θ+α)(r,\ \theta + \alpha) .
