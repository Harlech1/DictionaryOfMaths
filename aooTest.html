Euclidean geometry

It is the geometry of flat planes, straight lines and points.

(postulatları) Euclidean axioms (postulates)

Every two points determine exactly one straight line. A segment may be extended arbitrarily far in a straight line.

A circle may be drawn with any center and any radius. All right angles are the same.

Given any line and any point not on that line, there is exactly one line through the point which is parallel to the original line.
