For the ellipse $\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}} + \ \frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}\ = 1$ , a > b , the equation of the auxiliary circle is x2 + y2 = a2 .

For the ellipse $\frac{(x - h)^{2}}{a^{2}} + \ \frac{(y - k)^{2}}{b^{2}}\ = 1$ , a > b , the equation of the auxiliary circle is (x−h)2 + (y−k)2 = a2.

asal sayılar prime numbers; primes

A counting number , different from 1 , which is divisible only by 1 and by itself. P = {2, 3, 5 , 7 , 11 , 13 , 17, . . . }

The number of primes is infinite. The unique even prime is 2. All other primes are odd.

Every prime of the form p = 4n + 1 is a sum of two squares in one unique way:

2 = 12 + 12, 5 = 12 + 22, 13 = 22 + 32, 17 = 12 + 42, 29 = 22 + 52, 37 = 12 + 62, 41 = 42 + 52

A curve approaching a given curve arbitrarily closely.

düşey asimptot vertical asymptote

If y →  + ∞ or y →  − ∞ as x → m (the graph of y = f(x) approaches a vertical line x = m ), then the line x = m is called a vertical asymptote for f .

eğik asimptot oblique asymptote

If the graph of y = f(x) approaches a horizontal line y = mx + n as x →  + ∞ or x →  − ∞, then the line

y = mx + n is called an oblique asymptote for f. $If\ f(x) = \ \frac{N(X)}{D(X)}$ where N(x)and D(x) are polynomials and the degree of N (x) is 1 more than the degree of D(x) , then f(x) has an oblique asymptote .

$f(x)\ = \ \frac{x^{2} - 1}{x + 1} = x - 2 + \ \frac{3}{x + 2}$ . as x → ∞, f(x) approaches the line y = x − 2 which is the oblique asymptote

eğri asimptot curved asymptote

The parabola y= x2 is the curved asymptote of the graph of f(x)= x2 + (1/x) .

yatay asimptot horizontal asymptote

If the graph of y = f(x) approaches a horizontal line y = c ( c ∈ R) as x →  + ∞ or x →  − ∞ , then the line y = c

is called a horizontal asymptote for f. If a rational function is proper, that is if the degree of the numerator is less than the degree of the denominator, then the line y = 0 is a horizontal asymptote of its graph .

azalan dizi decreasing sequence; decreasing progression

For a sequence {an} , if an + 1 − an < 0 for n ≥ x, then {an} is decreasing for n ≥ x .

If an > 0 and $\frac{a_{n + 1}}{a_{n}} < 1$ for all n ≥ x, then {an} is decreasing for n ≥ x .

azalan fonksiyon decreasing function

If a function is decreasing, then, as the values of x get bigger, the values of the function get smaller.

A function f is decreasing on an interval I if ∀x1, x2  ∈ I , x2 > x1  ⇒ f(x2) ≤ f(x1 ).

If f is continuous on [a, b] and f′ exists and is negative everywhere on (a,b),then f is decreasing on [a, b].

mutlak azalan fonksiyon strictly decreasing function

A function f is strictly decreasing on an interval I if ∀x1, x2  ∈ I , x2 > x1  ⇒ f(x2) < f(x1 ).

azalan seri decreasing series

A series s1, s2, s3, . . is decreasing if s1 ≥ s2 ≥ s3 . . .sn ≥ sn + 1

azalan sıra descending order; decreasing order

We say that the polynomial p(x) = x3 + 3x2 − 4x + 2 is written in decreasing order (the exponent of x is decreasing from left to right).
