rhombus pl.pl.\ rhombi; diamond

A parallelogram with four congruent sides.

Opposite angles are congruent and adjacent angles are supplementary.

Each diagonal of a rhombus bisects the angles of the rhombus.

The diagonals of a rhombus are perpendicular and they bisect each other.

If the length of each side is a, and the lengths of the diagonals are e and f, then
