binomial distribution

The binomial distribution gives the probability distribution of obtaining exactly n successes out of n trials where the result of each trial is true with probability p and false with probability q=1−pq = 1 - p.

The formula for the binomial distribution is given by P(n∖N)=(Nn)pn.qN−nP(n\backslash N) = \left( \begin{array}{r} N \\ n \end{array} \right)p^{n}.q^{N - n}.
