Let AmxnA_{mxn} and BnxpB_{nxp} be two matrices such that the number of columns of A is equal to the number of rows of B , then the product ABB is defined as :

AmxnBnxp=CmxpA_{mxn}B_{nxp} = C_{mxp} where cik=∑j=1naijbjkc_{ik} = \sum_{j = 1}^{n}{a_{ij}b_{jk}}, i=1,2,3,…mi = 1,2,3,\ldots m\ and k=1,2,3,…,pk = 1,2,3,\ldots,p
