orthogonal projection

A projection of a figure by parallel rays. In such a projection;

I. parallel lines project to parallel lines,

II. projection lines are perpendicular to the plane of image,

III. the ratio of lengths of parallel segments and and the ratio of areas are preserved,

IV. the medians of a triangle project to the medians of the image triangle,

V. the centroid of the triangle projects to the centroid of the image triangle,

VI. any triangle can be positioned such that its image is equilateral,

VII. ellipses image to ellipses,

VII. the center of an ellipse projects to the center of the image ellipse,

VIII. any ellipse can be positioned such that its image is a circle, etc.
