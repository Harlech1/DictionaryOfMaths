focal parameter

The distance pp from the focus to the directrix of a conic section.

I. For an ellipse: p=b2a2−b2p = \frac{b^{2}}{\sqrt{a^{2} - b^{2}}} or p=a2−c2cp = \frac{a^{2} - c^{2}}{c} =a(1−e2)e= \frac{{a(1 - e}^{2})}{e} II. For a parabola: p=2ap = 2a

III. For a hyperbola: p=b2a2+b2p = \frac{b^{2}}{\sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}} or p=c2−a2cp = \frac{{c^{2} - a}^{2}}{c} =a(e2−1)e= \frac{{a(e}^{2} - 1)}{e}
