eş şekiller congruent figures

Two geometric figures that have exactly the same size and shape.

Two geometric figures are congruent if one can be transformed into the other by rotation and translation.

All pairs of corresponding sides and corresponding angles of congruent figures are congruent.

eş üçgenler congruent triangles

Two triangles are congruent if all their corresponding sides and corresponding angles are equal.

eşlik teoremleri congruency theorems

Angle-side-angle (ASA) congruence postulate açı-kenar -açı (AKA) eşlik postulatı

If two angles and the included side of one triangle are congruent to two angles and the included side of another

triangle , then the two triangles are congruent.

Angle-angle-side (AAS) congruence theorem açı-açı-kenar (AAK) eşlik teoremi

If two angles and a nonincluded side of one triangle are congruent to two angles and the corresponding nonincluded

side of another triangle, then the triangles are congruent.

HL congruence postulate If the hypothenuse and a leg of one right triangle are congruent to the hypothenuse and a

leg of another right triangle, then the right triangles are congruent.

LL congruence postulate If the legs of a right triangle are congruent to the legs of another triangle, then the right

triangles are congruent.

Side-side-side (SSS) congruence postulate kenar-kenar-kenar (KKK) eşlik postulatı
