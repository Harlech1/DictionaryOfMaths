test for symmetry

a) A graph is symmetric with respect to the x-axis if, ∀(x,y)\forall(x,y) on the graph, the point (x,−y)(x, - y) is also on the graph.

b) A graph is symmetric with respect to the y-axis if, ∀(x,y)\forall(x,y) on the graph, the point (−x,y)( - x,y) is also on the graph.

c) A graph is symmetric with respect to the origin if, ∀(x,y)\forall(x,y) on the graph, the point (−x,−y)( - x, - y) is also on the graph.
