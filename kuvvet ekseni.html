radical axis; radical line (of two circles)

The locus of points of equal power with respect to two nonconcentric circles which is perpendicular to the line of centers.

If two circles are intersecting in two points, then the radical axis is the line passing through the points of intersection.

If two circles are tangent to each other, then the radical axis is the line passing through the point of tangency and perpendicular to the line passing through the centers of the circles .
