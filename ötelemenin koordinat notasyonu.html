coordinate notation for translation

A translation of a point (x,y)(x,y) is defined by (x,y)→(x+h,y+k)(x,y) \rightarrow (x + h,y + k) where hh indicates the number of units the point moves horizontally and kk indicates the number of units the point moves vertically .

It is the composition of a glide (translation) and a reflection in a line parallel to the glide vector.
