isometry; congrunce transformation

A transformation that preserves length and angle measure.

An isometry is a transformation in which the preimage and the image are congruent.

There are only four isometries : reflection, translation, rotation, and glide reflection .

composition theorem

The composition of two (or more) isometries is an isometry.

isometric
