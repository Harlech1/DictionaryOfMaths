cube

The three-dimensional Platonic solid. The uniform polyhedron with 6 congruent square faces.

It is a right prism, also called hexahedron. The centers of the faces of a cube form an octahedron.

The cross section of a cube may be a square, hexagon or other shape.

Each face is perpendicular to the faces which are adjacent to it. It has a dihedral angle of 90090^{0}.

The segment which form the faces are called edges. The edges meet at the vertices.

It has 8 vertices and 12 congruent edges. The length of an edge: a
