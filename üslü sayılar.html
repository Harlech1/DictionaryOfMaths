exponentials

In an expression like ama^{m}, the aa is called the basebase, and the mm is the exponentexponent or powerpower.

a2a^{2} read as ‘’aa to the second power’’ or ‘’ aa raised to the second power’’ or ‘’aa squared’’.

a3a^{3} read as ‘’aa to the third power’’ or ‘’ aa raised to the third power’’ or ‘’aa cubed’’.
