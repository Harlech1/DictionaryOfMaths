sum of the divisors

If the counting number nn is written as n=p1m1p2m2p3m3...pkmkn = {p_{1}}^{m_{1}}{{p}_{2}}^{m_{2}}{p_{3}}^{m_{3}}...{p_{k}}^{m_{k}} , where pmp^{m} is a prime number which

divides nn , then the sum of the positive integer divisors of nn is :

Sn=(1+p1+p12+...+p1m1)(1+p2+p22+...+p2m2)...(1+pk+pk2+...+pkmk){S}_{n} = \left( 1 + p_{1} + {p_{1}}^{2} + ... + {p_{1}}^{m_{1}} \right)\left( 1 + p_{2} + {p_{2}}^{2} + ... + {p_{2}}^{m_{2}} \right)...\left( 1 + p_{k} + {p_{k}}^{2} + ... + {p_{k}}^{m_{k}} \right)
