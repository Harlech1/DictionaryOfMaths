orthocenter (of a triangle)

The lines containing the altitudes of a triangle are concurrent at a point called

the orthocenter of the triangle.

I. If the the triangle is acute, its orthocenter is in the interior of the triangle.

II. If the triangle is obtuse, its orthocenter is in the exterior of the triangle.

III. If the triangle is a right triangle, its orthocenter is the vertex of the right angle.
